Professor (Principal Investigator)
I'm a professor in Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Graduate School of AI, and Dept. of Applied Data Science at the Sungkyunkwan University. I work at the intersection of natural language processing (NLP) and information retrieval (IR) based on Language Models, and I am interested in NLP (dialogue system, question/answering system, etc.), IR (GraphRAG, etc.), Generative AI (controllable text generation, etc.), Machine learning for AI (reinforcement learning, etc), and Text Mining (text classification/summarization, sentiment classification, etc.) for Agentic and Physical AI.
